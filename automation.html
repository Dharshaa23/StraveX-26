Track 03

Smart Automation

Designing AI-driven, sensor-based, and data-intelligent systems that automate real-world processes to improve safety, efficiency, and decision-making.

10 Problem Statements
Max 5 Members
18 March 2026
Problem Statements

Choose Your Challenge

Select any one problem statement from below. Build intelligent automation systems that solve real, high-impact challenges.

01
Computer Vision / Safety

Automated Construction Site Safety Monitor

Construction sites face frequent safety violations. Workers often ignore helmets and safety protocols, and supervisors cannot manually monitor all zones simultaneously. This leads to accidents and regulatory penalties.

Problem Statement
Build a computer vision system to detect safety gear compliance on construction sites and send real-time alerts to supervisors when violations are detected.
02
AI / Career Guidance

AI-Based Smart Job Role Recommendation System

Students are confused about suitable career paths and lack personalized career guidance. Generic advice does not account for individual skills, academic performance, or personal interests.

Problem Statement
Build an AI model that analyzes skills, interests, and academic records to recommend optimal job roles and career development paths for students.
03
IoT / Disaster Management

Automated Smart Street Flood Detection System

Urban flooding disrupts traffic and public safety. Authorities receive delayed information about waterlogging, making emergency response inefficient and reactive rather than proactive.

Problem Statement
Design a water-level sensor network that sends real-time flood alerts and predicts risk zones using historical data to enable proactive disaster management.
04
AI / Traffic

Smart Dynamic Traffic Signal Control System

Traditional traffic signals operate on fixed timers and do not adapt to real-time traffic conditions. This leads to congestion on busy roads while adjacent lanes remain empty and underutilized.

Problem Statement
Design an intelligent traffic signal system that dynamically adjusts signal timing based on real-time vehicle density at intersections to reduce congestion and wait times.
05
IoT / Energy

Smart Appliance-Level Energy Monitoring System

Energy wastage in homes and institutions increases electricity bills and carbon emissions. Users lack awareness of real-time appliance-level power consumption, making it hard to identify and eliminate waste.

Problem Statement
Build a system that monitors appliance-level electricity usage in real time and provides actionable energy-saving suggestions to users through a dashboard or mobile app.
06
Hardware / Security

Intelligent Electric Fence Tamper Detection System

Electric fences used in agricultural lands, wildlife reserves, and restricted areas lack intelligent monitoring. Unauthorized tampering goes undetected, compromising security, safety, and livestock protection.

Problem Statement
Design a hardware-based intelligent system that can detect, prevent, and report unauthorized use or tampering of electric fences in real time by integrating monitoring, authentication, and alert mechanisms.
07
AI / GIS / Safety

Smart Disaster Evacuation Assistant

During disasters, people panic and lack real-time evacuation guidance. Existing alert systems do not provide personalized, location-aware safe-route navigation to individuals caught in affected zones.

Problem Statement
Develop a mobile app integrated with GIS data to suggest real-time safe evacuation routes using AI, considering current disaster zones, population density, and road conditions.
08
RFID / Healthcare

Smart Pharmacy Inventory Expiry Manager

Expired medicines cause financial loss and serious health risks. Manual tracking of expiry dates is error-prone, especially in pharmacies managing thousands of SKUs across multiple batches.

Problem Statement
Develop a barcode/RFID-based inventory system for pharmacies that sends automated alerts before medicine expiry and suggests stock rotation strategies to minimize waste.
09
AI / CCTV / Security

Smart Facial Recognition CCTV Security System

Traditional CCTV systems cannot differentiate between family members and strangers, leading to ineffective monitoring and delayed intrusion response in residential and commercial spaces.

Problem Statement
Build an AI-powered CCTV system with facial recognition that identifies authorized individuals and sends instant alerts when an unknown person is detected inside or near the premises.
10
IoT / AI / Predictive Maintenance

IoT-Enabled Vocational Lab Equipment Monitor

Vocational training labs rely on machinery for hands-on education but face challenges including improper equipment usage, delayed maintenance, unexpected breakdowns, and poor utilization tracking — leading to safety risks and reduced training quality.

Problem Statement
Design a scalable IoT & AI-enabled monitoring system that tracks equipment usage in real time, detects faults and unsafe conditions, enables predictive maintenance, and provides actionable dashboards for trainers and administrators in vocational training labs.
