Track 05

Inclusive & Accessible Systems

Engineering low-cost, human-centred solutions that empower individuals with disabilities and ensure that technology serves everyone equally.

10 Problem Statements
Max 5 Members
18 March 2026
Problem Statements

Choose Your Challenge

Select any one problem statement from below. Design for empathy — build systems that make the world more accessible for everyone.

01
AI / Sign Language

Real-Time AI Sign Language Translation System

Deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals face significant communication barriers in everyday interactions. There is no affordable, real-time bridge between sign language and spoken or written communication.

Problem Statement
Design a real-time AI-powered sign language recognition and translation system that converts sign language into text/speech and vice versa during live interactions.
02
Computer Vision / Navigation

Indoor Navigation System for Visually Impaired

Visually impaired individuals face significant challenges navigating indoor spaces such as hospitals, offices, and educational institutions where GPS signals are unreliable or unavailable.

Problem Statement
Develop an indoor navigation system using computer vision, Bluetooth beacons, or ultrasonic sensors that provides real-time audio guidance for visually impaired users.
03
Environment Design / Education

Neuro-Inclusive Classroom Environment Design

Students with dyslexia, ADHD, or autism often struggle in standard classroom environments not designed for their sensory and cognitive needs, significantly impacting their learning outcomes.

Problem Statement
Create a physically optimized classroom environment — considering lighting, acoustics, seating arrangement, and sensory zones — that supports students with dyslexia, ADHD, or autism for better focus and learning.
04
Accessibility / Civic Tech

Voice-Assisted Accessible Digital Voting Interface

Current digital voting systems are inaccessible to individuals with physical or visual disabilities. They require fine motor control and visual literacy, effectively excluding a large portion of the electorate.

Problem Statement
Engineer a secure, voice-assisted and gesture-controlled digital voting interface that ensures complete independence and privacy during the voting process for users with disabilities.
05
AAC / Mental Health

AAC-Based Mental Health Companion App

Individuals with non-verbal communication challenges or severe anxiety often cannot express emotions or seek mental health support through standard interfaces. Existing apps are not accessible to them.

Problem Statement
Implement an AAC-based mental health companion app that uses symbol boards, eye-tracking, or switch access to help users express emotions and receive appropriate mental health support.
06
Hardware / Assistive Device

Low-Cost Wheelchair Mobility Assistive Attachment

Mobility-impaired students in wheelchairs struggle to navigate uneven terrains, staircases, or narrow corridors in college campuses. Existing powered solutions are far too expensive for most institutions.

Problem Statement
Innovate a low-cost, manually operable assistive device or attachment for wheelchairs that helps mobility-impaired students navigate uneven terrains, staircases, or narrow college corridors independently.
07
AI / HR / Accessibility

Disability-Inclusive Accessible Hiring Platform

Standard hiring platforms and interview processes are inaccessible to candidates with disabilities. The lack of adaptive UI and alternative assessment formats excludes talented individuals from the workforce.

Problem Statement
Craft a fully accessible hiring platform that includes adaptive UI, AI interview tools with alternative formats (text, ASL, voice), and disability-inclusive skill assessments.
08
Hardware / Emergency Safety

Emergency Evacuation Plan for Disabled Individuals

Most emergency evacuation plans in public buildings are designed for able-bodied individuals. Disabled persons have no specific structural, mechanical, or alerting systems designed to support their needs.

Problem Statement
Formulate a structural and mechanical emergency evacuation plan with physical aids — such as evacuation chairs, tactile floor guides, and vibration-based alert panels — designed specifically for disabled individuals in public buildings.
09
AI / Healthcare Communication

Accessible Medical Document Converter

Medical documents are written in complex language inaccessible to patients with low literacy, cognitive disabilities, or language barriers. This prevents informed medical consent and patient empowerment.

Problem Statement
Transform how medical documents are consumed by converting them into plain language, audio, pictograms, or regional languages in real time, ensuring informed consent for all patients.
10
Hardware / Public Infrastructure

Low-Cost Retrofit Accessibility System for Public Transport

Public buses and train stations in India largely lack accessible infrastructure for disabled users. Major reconstruction is often not feasible, but practical retrofitting solutions have been overlooked.

Problem Statement
Revolutionize public transport accessibility by designing low-cost ramp systems, tactile pathways, and ergonomic boarding aids for buses and train stations that can be retrofitted into existing infrastructure without major reconstruction.
