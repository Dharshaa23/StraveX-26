Track 01

Campus Micro-Systems

Identifying and solving overlooked operational, logistical, and convenience challenges that exist within a college campus environment.

10 Problem Statements
Max 5 Members
18 March 2026
Problem Statements

Choose Your Challenge

Select any one problem statement from below. Read carefully, identify the core issue, and build an intelligent solution.

01
Academic Support

Peer Academic Support Platform

Many students struggle in certain subjects but hesitate to ask for help. There is no structured platform on campus to connect students who need academic support with those who can provide it.

Problem Statement
Create a platform where students can list subjects they need help with and connect with peers who can provide academic support.
02
Library Management

Smart Library Book Availability System

In many college libraries, students spend a lot of time manually searching for books. Books are often unavailable, misplaced, or already issued, causing confusion and time wastage.

Problem Statement
Develop a simple application that allows users to search books and check their real-time availability status.
03
Energy Monitoring

Campus Electricity Usage Tracker

Colleges record electricity consumption manually without proper tracking or analysis. This makes it difficult to monitor monthly usage and identify areas of excessive power consumption.

Problem Statement
Design a system where daily electricity meter readings can be entered manually and monthly consumption can be monitored using interactive charts.
04
Transport

Real-Time Campus Bus Tracker

Students often wait for college buses without knowing the exact arrival time or current location. This leads to unnecessary waiting and inconvenience, especially during peak hours.

Problem Statement
Develop a system where the bus driver can update the current location/status, and students can view it in real time.
05
Scheduling

Smart Timetable & Class Reminder App

Students sometimes miss classes or forget schedule changes due to lack of proper reminders. Printed timetables are not dynamic and cannot push notifications for last-minute changes.

Problem Statement
Build an application that displays the class timetable and sends smart reminders for upcoming classes and schedule changes.
06
Campus Services

Lost & Found Campus Portal

When students lose personal belongings on campus, there is no centralized system to report or search for lost items. This significantly reduces the chances of recovering lost property.

Problem Statement
Create an online portal where students can report lost or found items and search for matching entries using descriptions or categories.
07
Infrastructure

Campus Parking Availability Display

Students and staff face difficulty finding available parking spaces inside the campus, leading to congestion and time loss. There is no system to display real-time parking availability.

Problem Statement
Develop a system that displays available and occupied parking slots in real time, with a manual-update mechanism for attendants.
08
Canteen

Digital Canteen Pre-Order System

During break times, long queues form in the college canteen, causing delays and inconvenience. There is no system for advance ordering, leading to crowding and food wastage.

Problem Statement
Design a web or mobile-based system that allows students to view the menu, place pre-orders, and receive a token number for pickup.
09
Administration

Student Complaint Management Platform

Students face issues related to infrastructure, facilities, or academics but often have no proper channel to register and track complaints. Manual complaint systems lack transparency and accountability.

Problem Statement
Create a centralized digital platform where students can submit complaints and track their status, and administrators can manage and update them.
10
Examination

Automated Exam Seating Arrangement System

Classroom and seating arrangements are often done manually during exams, leading to confusion and errors. The process is time-consuming for administrators and prone to mistakes.

Problem Statement
Develop an application that automatically assigns students to classrooms and seats using their register numbers or department details.
