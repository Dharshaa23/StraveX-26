Track 04

Smart Education

Reimagining the educational experience through AI, automation, and data-driven platforms that personalise learning, improve outcomes, and reduce inefficiencies.

10 Problem Statements
Max 5 Members
18 March 2026
Problem Statements

Choose Your Challenge

Select any one problem statement from below. Build intelligent education systems that personalise learning and reduce friction.

01
AI / Placement Prep

AI-Based Placement Preparation System

Many college students struggle with placement preparation. Students lack personalized mock tests, aptitude training, and interview feedback, leading to poor performance during campus recruitment.

Problem Statement
Build an AI-based system providing aptitude practice, coding tests, and AI-powered mock interviews with personalized feedback to help students prepare for campus placements.
02
AI / Internships

Smart Internship & Industry Matching Platform

Students find it difficult to get relevant internships due to skill mismatch between candidates and companies. Generic job boards do not match students based on academic profile or domain interests.

Problem Statement
Build an AI platform that matches students with relevant internship opportunities based on skills, CGPA, interests, and career goals.
03
AI / Research

Digital Research Paper Recommendation System

College students need research papers for projects but find it hard to discover relevant and authentic research articles. Search engines return overwhelming or irrelevant results.

Problem Statement
Build an AI-based system that recommends relevant, authentic research papers to students based on subject, keywords, and academic level.
04
ML / Academic Analytics

Smart Academic Performance Prediction System

Some students fail due to poor preparation and there is no early warning system for low-performing students. By the time results are out, it is often too late to intervene effectively.

Problem Statement
Build a machine learning model that predicts semester performance using attendance data and internal marks to provide early warnings and support recommendations to faculty and students.
05
AI / Productivity

Smart Time Management Planner for Students

Students struggle to balance studies, internships, and extracurricular activities. Poor time planning and the lack of structured scheduling reduces productivity and increases academic stress.

Problem Statement
Build an AI planner that intelligently schedules study hours, assignment deadlines, and exam preparation based on a student's timetable and personal priorities.
06
AI / Skills

Skill Gap Analysis & Course Recommendation Platform

Students lack industry-required skills and there is no system to identify skill gaps before placement. Students are unaware of what they need to learn to become truly job-ready.

Problem Statement
Build an AI-based skill assessment platform that identifies a student's skill gaps relative to industry requirements and recommends targeted courses and resources to close them.
07
AI / Examination

Automated Exam Paper Generation System

Teachers spend significant time creating balanced question papers. Manual creation may lack difficulty balance, consistent syllabus coverage, and prevention of question repetition across different sets.

Problem Statement
Build an intelligent exam generator that categorizes questions by difficulty, ensures full syllabus coverage, prevents repetition, and generates multiple balanced question sets automatically.
08
Computer Vision / Attendance

Smart Attendance System Using Face Recognition

Calling out names for attendance consumes 10–15 minutes per class session. Manual attendance is susceptible to proxy marking and paper-based records make it difficult to track long-term attendance trends.

Problem Statement
Build an automated system using a classroom camera that identifies students through facial recognition as they enter or sit, marks attendance instantly, and updates a digital database in real time.
09
AI / Alumni Networking

AI-Based Alumni Networking Platform

Alumni connections significantly help in career growth, but students lack a proper alumni interaction platform. Existing systems are generic and do not match students with the most relevant alumni mentors.

Problem Statement
Build an AI platform that intelligently connects students with relevant alumni based on career interests, domain of study, and professional goals to facilitate meaningful mentorship.
10
AI / Academic Integrity

Smart Assignment Submission & Plagiarism Checker

Manual assignment tracking is inefficient and plagiarism is increasingly common. There is no centralized, automated system to manage submissions and ensure the originality of student work.

Problem Statement
Build an online assignment submission system with built-in plagiarism detection that flags copied content, generates similarity reports, and notifies faculty automatically.
